The Jabbawockeez are a world-famous dance crew who first gained fame in their national television appearance on America’s Got Talent in 2006, before winning the MTV Hip Hop reality series, Randy Jackson Presents America’s Best Dance Crew (ABDC) in March 2008. The most notable group to come out of the MTV reality series, the Jabbawockeez are synonymous with the show and universally recognized as America’s true best dance crew.

The Kinjaz in full performance mode. The Kinjaz uniquely combines music and urban-dancing to create original and entertaining forms of story-telling through the group's fusion of theatrics, intricate choreography, and comedy, which is as spell-binding as it is skillful.

Nicknamed Lil Beats and Ca Blaze, Les Twins have danced for everyone from Beyonce to Missy Elliott, even winning World of Dance, the reality dance competition produced by Jennifer Lopez. Now, they travel the world doing workshops and teaching their original choreography.p>

The Tropical Gem dance company was founded in May 2000 by Rafael Gonzalez and Fernando Sosa. The team has participated in the biggest World and International Congresses. The choreographies of Tropical Gem amount to more than 50. Winners of the most prestigious salsa group competitions in the world such as Las Vegas Mega Salsa Crew.

Adolfo Indacochea and his Latin Soul Dancer are an explosive and energetic group known worldwide for their smooth and unique style. This unique style combines traditional mambo with elements of jazz/modern, flamenco and ballroom dancing. Its sense of musicality and flow is evident through its choreography and improvisations.

Full Project Company is a group born 10 years ago, initially as a duo made up of the two dance masters Luigi Bellomo and Luigi Bianco, who proposed for the first time in Italy and Europe an unusual male salsa couple. With this formation they were twice world champions in pasitos and worked on Costa Crociere ships and in the films 'Street dance 2' and 'The latin dream'. The Caribbean dance world is 60% female and two years ago, convinced that they were offering the market something new, they held a casting to create a group of male-only salsa dancers.
